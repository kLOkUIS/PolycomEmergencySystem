Power measurement notes (2026-04-13)
===================================

Context
- Goal: isolate sleep behavior before reintegrating with knob firmware.
- Board/software flashed: RAK4630LEDTEST.ino at repo root (timer + button wake test variant).

New measurements reported
- Shunt resistor: 10 ohm
- Scope reading while asleep (average): about 2.5 mV
- Equivalent current (Ohm's law): about 250 uA

Probe/scope attenuation observation
- With 10x probe/scope setup: about 2.5 mV observed
- With 1x setting: about 4 mV observed
- Suspicion: probe attenuation and scope channel attenuation may not have matched in one run.

Comparison to earlier reference
- Earlier reference used 100 ohm shunt and about 2.1 mV average (about 21 uA)
- Apparent current difference vs new 10 ohm run is about 10x to 20x, likely influenced by setup/method differences.

Recommended check before comparing runs
- Verify probe and scope channel attenuation match (both 1x or both 10x).
- Keep identical scope averaging/timebase and wiring between A/B runs.
- Keep J-Link disconnected during low-power measurements.
