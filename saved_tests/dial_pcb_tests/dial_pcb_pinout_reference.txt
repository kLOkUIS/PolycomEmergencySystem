Dial PCB Pinout Reference

LEDS
- P0_24: LED_RX
- P0_31: LED_DIAL_CTL

KEY_CTL (TMUX1511 control lines)
- P1_01: KEY1_CTL (Back-Up)
- P1_02: KEY2_CTL (1)
- P1_03: KEY3_CTL (Talk/End)
- P1_04: KEY4_CTL (Vol+)
- P0_05: KEY5_CTL (2)
- P0_04: KEY6_CTL (3)

CALL_SENSE
- P0_25: CALL_SENSE

UART
- P0_20: UART1_TX
- P0_19: UART1_RX

Notes
- KEY_CTL lines should default LOW at boot (no key pressed).
- CALL_SENSE is externally pulled up; LOW means call LED active.
